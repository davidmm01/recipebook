## Ingredients
- neutral oil
- 2 packets of Lee Kum Kee mapo tofu sauce
- 1 brown onion, sliced finely

- 1 knob of ginger, grated/sliced/chopped
- 1 tablespoon oyster sauce
- 2 teaspoons soy sauce
- 500g pork mince

- spring onions (garnish, optional)
