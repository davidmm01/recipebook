Sauce For Fried Rice

Sep 18, 2023 sauce rice

Ingredients

  • 1/3 cup Soy sauce
  • 1/3 cup Oyster sauce
  • 1/3 cup Mirin
  • 1/4 cup Sesame oil
  • 1 tablespoon white pepper

Instructions

  • Combine all the ingredients in a jar. Keep sealed and store in the fridge.
