Tomato Egg Prawn Stirfry

Dec 1, 2023 chinese prawn egg tomato rice

Ingredients 3 very large tomatoes 500g prawns 6 eggs 5 cloves of garlic, minced or chopped 4 spring onions, sliced and separated in two, half ingredient half garnish 2 tbsp oyster sauce 2 tsp sesame oil 2 tsp sugar 2 tsp chicken stock 1/2 tsp chicken salt 1/2 tsp pepper 2 tbsp corn starch 4 tbsp water Instructions Put the rice cooker on in advance Mix together the sauce by combining oyster sauce, sesame oil, sugar, chicken stock, salt and pepper.
