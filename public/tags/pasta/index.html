Chilli Wine Garlic Prawn Pasta

Dec 19, 2023 pasta prawn

Ingredients 1kg prawns 360g linguine or similar 3 shallot, diced or half a white onion 2 red chilli, deseeded and diced 400 grams cherry tomatoes, halved 5 cloves garlic, finely sliced 1 cup white wine, 2 tbsp unsalted butter zest of 2 lemon 2 tbsp fresh chopped parsley 40 grams grated parmesan cheese plus extra for serving if desired Instructions Bring a large pot of salted water to boil for cooking the pasta.
