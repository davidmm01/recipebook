Eel

Unagi Sauce

Sep 14, 2023 sauce eel

Ingredients 60ml mirin 1½ Tbsp sake 2½ Tbsp sugar 60ml soy sauce Instructions In a small saucepan, add the mirin, sake and sugar. Turn on the heat to medium and whisk all the ingredients together. Add soy sauce and bring to a boil. Once boiling, reduce the heat to low and continue simmering for 10 minutes. Toward the end of cooking, you will see more bubbles. Turn off the heat and let it cool.
