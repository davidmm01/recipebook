Lamb Shanks

Lamb Shanks Massaman Curry

Sep 18, 2023 curry lamb shanks

Ingredients 1.5kg lamb shanks (5 small, 4 medium, 2 - 3 large) 114g/ 4oz Maesri Massaman curry paste (1 can), or other brand (Note 1) 400ml coconut milk, full fat (Ayam brand is best, Note 3) 2 cups chicken stock/broth 1 onion 400g small potatoes 1 star anise 1 cinnamon stick Red chilli, finely sliced (garnish) Coriander (garnish) Instructions Preheat oven to 180°C (160°C fan). Mix curry paste, coconut milk and stock in a baking dish.
