Margarita

2025-09-28 00:00:00 +0000 UTC

Tags: tequila lime salt
Cuisines: string

Ingredients

  • juice of 1 lime
  • 45ml tequila (blanco or reposado)
  • 15ml cointreu
  • 15ml agave syrup
  • pinch of salt

Instructions

  • Combine all ingredients.
  • Shake
  • Strain and serve.
  • Optionally serve ice and or a splash of soda water.
